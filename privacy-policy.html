2. Third-Party Data Collection (Epic Online Services)

Project Sync integrates with Epic Online Services (EOS) for authentication, matchmaking, and related features. EOS may collect and process the following data under their own privacy policy:

  • Account identifiers and authentication tokens
  • Player data including usernames, session details, and matchmaking metrics
  • Device identifiers and network information (e.g., IP address)

For details on how EOS handles your data, please see the EOS Privacy Policy: https://dev.epicgames.com/terms/privacy

3. Security of Your Information
